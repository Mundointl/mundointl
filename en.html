About Us

Mundo International Co., Ltd. is a Korea-based export company dedicated to bringing high-quality Korean products to international markets. Since 2020, we have been exporting worldwide, building strong partnerships grounded in reliability, transparency, and long-term collaboration.

We act not only as a supplier but as a strategic partner—connecting overseas buyers directly with competitive Korean manufacturers to ensure stable supply, efficient communication, and sustainable growth.

Our Services

We specialize exclusively in export operations from Korea, taking full responsibility for international distribution and providing professional business consulting support.

Our key sectors include automotive products, food & beverage, cosmetics, and a wide range of industrial and consumer goods. Through a direct-from-manufacturer model, we eliminate unnecessary intermediaries to offer competitive pricing and trusted quality.

Our vision is simple: to become your most reliable gateway to Korea across all industries.
